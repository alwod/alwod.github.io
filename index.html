Portfolio

Here are some of the projects I have worked on, some on my own and others as part of a team. These are not all of my projects however, feel free to visit my github page to see all the things that I've made. Some of my projects were made as part of a university course and have not been uploaded to a public repository. If you want to see these, such as my physics engine and parallelisation of the 8-queens problem, please send me an e-mail.

VR Shooter

Made for my honours project, the player must find their way through a simple maze, shooting at targets at several stations throughout. Made using Unity.

Maze Escape

A game made by me and Liam Blair using a custom game engine in C++. Play as an Atari-style explorer who must find their way through the maze before time runs out!

Back to Roots

Made with a team during the 2023 Global Game Jam. A top-down 'shooter' where you must go back to your roots, finding your family in a dark foresst...

Procedural City Generator

For my Advanced Games Engineering module at Uni, I decided to create a procedural city generator in Unity, utilising Voronoi diagrams to create a large city with various districts and roads.

Mr Weiner's Wish [IN PROGRESS]

For the 2024 Global Game Jam, me and Cade Brown sought to create a 3D platformer with unique controls. You play as a Dachshund called Mr Weiner, who must eat food to increase his length, allowing him to grapple to surfaces like door handles!

About Me

Hi! My name is Mikha Röhrs and I am a programmer and game designer. I studied Games Development at Edinburgh Napier University where I achieved a bachelors of science honours degree (2:1). I also studied computing at West Lothian College, where I got an A for my HNC degree.

I have been deeply interested in games since I was a child, when I'd constantly make my own board games and get my friends to play them. Using the knowledge I have gained during the past 6 years I now aim to create video games and share them to players all around the world.
